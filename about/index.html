About

I'm a Data Science graduate from UC San Diego (B.S., June 2026), and I like the whole pipeline — from wrangling raw, messy data to shipping something people can actually use. Lately that's meant building analytics tools and machine learning systems that turn open questions into answers a team can act on.

I work best iteratively and hands-on: I'd rather sketch a rough version, put it in front of people, and sharpen it than plan forever. That habit shows up in how I approach data problems too — start with a defensible baseline, measure, then improve the part that matters.
