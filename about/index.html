About

I'm a Data Science graduate from UC San Diego (B.S., June 2026), and I like the whole pipeline — from wrangling raw, messy data to shipping something people can actually use. Lately that's meant building end-to-end RAG systems and analytics tools that turn open questions into answers a team can act on.

I work best iteratively and hands-on: I'd rather sketch a rough version, put it in front of people, and sharpen it than plan forever. That habit shows up in how I approach data problems too — start with a defensible baseline, measure, then improve the part that matters.

I'm looking for a role in data science, analytics, or AI engineering where I can keep building. Outside of work you'll find me watching soccer, playing padel or basketball, catching a movie, or talking cars.
